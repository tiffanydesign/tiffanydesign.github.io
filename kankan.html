Product Design

KANKAN

A City Selection and Life Planning Tool for Chinese Digital Nomads.

Role
Product Designer
Type
KTH Course Project
Year
2024
Background

The rise of remote work

The rise of remote work is fueling a mobile lifestyle across China. Yet, digital nomads lack tools to decide their next move, as essential information is fragmented across platforms, preventing trusted, side-by-side comparisons.

KANKAN — Landing Page
Problem

Core Pain Points

When choosing their next city, digital nomads commonly face:

Fragmented city information, making it hard to form a quick understanding
Uncertainty about which places fit their budget, safety needs, and lifestyle preferences
Lack of practical, on-the-ground information, such as where to stay, community vibe, and local norms

These issues make relocation inefficient and full of uncertainty.

Solution

A clear path forward

We break down the entire relocation process into three key steps:

1
Discover Cities
Use clear city tags to help users quickly grasp key highlights.
2
Choose a City
Filter and receive recommendations based on personal preferences.
3
Settle In & Live
Get answers and make plans through Q&A, resolving various questions before departure.

Design

Experience Highlights

Comparable City Information
From cost of living and climate to safety and community atmosphere, everything is clear at a glance.
Life-Oriented Support
Find instant answers to common practical questions, such as accommodation, cultural habits, and safety.
Insights from Real Users
Cities are presented not just as data points, but as places where real people have lived and shared their experiences.

Outcome

From chaos to clarity

KANKAN helps users transform the originally chaotic and uncertain city selection process into one that is more directed, evidence-based, and actionable. It turns "I don't know where to go" into "I know which places are right for me."
